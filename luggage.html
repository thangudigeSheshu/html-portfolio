Points to Remember:

  1. Passengers flying on Dream Flights domestic or international sectors on the same ticket will have the baggage allowance of the international sector applied.
  2. Passengers holding separate tickets for Dream Flights domestic and connecting international flights can take advantage of Free Baggage Allowance (FBA), if:
    • The connection flight is within 24 hours.
    • Both flights are operated by Dream Flights.
  3. Star Alliance Gold members can carry an additional 20 kg/44 lb of baggage in economy class.
  4. Members of premium clubs and our Flying Returns programme will get additional baggage.
  5. The maximum weight permissible for a single piece of baggage is 32 kg/71 lb. This rule applies to the entire Dream Flights network.
  6. Passengers can carry assistive devices free of charge as additional baggage subject to the limitation of the aircraft as per Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) guidelines.
