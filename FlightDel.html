Flight Delays & Cancellations

(1) Will I be contacted in advance if my flight is delayed, preponed or cancelled?

  • If you have provided us with your SMS-capable mobile phone number while booking, then Dream Flights will notify you in advance through an SMS on your mobile phone. Passengers will also be sent the revised itinerary on their registered email address.
  • We strongly recommend all passengers to provide us with their correct phone number and email address at the time of booking, in order to enable us to inform them in unforeseen cases of flight delays or cancellations.

(2) What is Dream Flights's policy if a flight is cancelled, delayed or preponed?

  • Dream Flights reserves the right to cancel, reschedule, or delay the commencement or continuance of a flight or to alter the stopping place or deviate from the route of the journey without incurring any liability in damages or otherwise to the Customers or any other person whatsoever. Sometimes circumstances beyond Dream Flights' control result in flight delays or cancellations. In such circumstances, Dream Flights reserves the right to cancel or delay a flight without prior notice. Circumstances beyond Dream Flights' control can include, without limitation, weather, air traffic control, mechanical failures, acts of terrorism, acts of nature, force majeure, strikes, riots, wars, hostilities, disturbances, governmental regulations, orders, demands, or requirements, shortages of critical manpower, parts, or materials, labor unrest, etc. Dream Flights does not connect to other airlines and is not responsible for any losses incurred by Customers while trying to connect to or from other airlines. In case of delays of more than 2 hours or Preponement of the flight by more than 1 hour, customers are entitled to a full refund or re-booking onto an alternative Dream Flights flight at no additional cost subject to availability. In the special case in which a subsequent portion of a Dream Flights flight is cancelled while a Customer is already in transit, a Customer shall have the right to choose to remain at the transit station and accept a partial refund for the portion of the flight not completed; or to return to the point of origin and receive a refund; or a credit for future travel on Dream Flights; or re-booking onto an alternative Dream Flights flight at no additional cost subject to availability. Any compensation arising out of cancellation or delay of flights will be processed as per DGCA guideline outlined in Civil Aviation Requirement, Section - 3, Series M Part IV. Passengers who have not provided valid contact information at the time of flight booking may not be entitled to any compensation.
  • In case of delay or cancellation of a flight operated as part of the Connected Segments/Route scheme, the following shall apply:
    1. In case of delay or cancellation of the connecting flight at the point of origin which leads to the possibility of a missed connection at the point of transit, the passenger shall have the right to choose a refund, or a credit for future travel on Dream Flights, or re-booking onto an alternative Dream Flights flight at no additional cost subject to availability.
    2. In case of a delay or cancellation of the connecting flight at the point of transit, Dream Flights will, at its discretion and subject to availability of seats, first offer the passenger the option to travel on the next Dream Flights flight, or that of another airline. If either of these options cannot materialize, Dream Flights will, at its discretion, provide lodging for an overnight stay to the passenger at the point of transit, ground transportation to and from the airport and hotel, and dinner.

(3) Will Dream Flights provide any additional benefits to passengers in case their flight is cancelled or rescheduled?

  • Dream FLights will not be liable for any loss incurred due to flight cancellation or rescheduling, and passengers will not be provided any additional benefits.
